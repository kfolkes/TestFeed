Problem

All throughout school, students of all ages struggle on test and continuously fail. With these failures teachers can only assess the situation after their students have already finished.

They do not have the chance to see if the problem is fulled based on anxiety, confusion, stress, or just pure lack of knowledge.

Solution

So to bridge the gap we have Test Feed. This resources will give teachers a chance to use real time facial recognition, and document trackers on their students test.

Test Feed will give teachers the ability to accurately pinpoint the problem, getting them closer to an overall solution.

How it was built

We used raspberrypi, and python api hat allowed up to implement document tracking

We used a camera pi to record and kairos for facial recognition
